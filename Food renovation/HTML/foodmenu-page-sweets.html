Sweets Food

Sweet Jalebi

Golden, coil-shaped batter deep-fried to shattering crispness, then dunked in saffron-infused sugar syrup—best served warm with a side of creamy rabri or chilled milk. A street food legend that’s impossible to resist!

Ingredients

  • 1 Rose syrup
  • 1 tsp Active dry yeast
  • 1 cup All purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp Corn starch
  • 10 drops Food color, Orange

Kaju katli

Silky-smooth cashew fudge, delicately flavored with cardamom and rose water, hand-cut into diamond-shaped jewels. A melt-in-your-mouth luxury that’s gluten-free and irresistibly elegant.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cup cashews powdered
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 4 inches silver vark
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon ghee

Gulab Jamun

Soft, melt-in-your-mouth dumplings made from khoya (milk solids) and flour, deep-fried to golden perfection, then soaked in warm cardamom-infused sugar syrup. A heavenly dessert that’s impossible to eat just one of!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Dry Milk Powder
  • 2 tablespoon Melted Ghee/ Butter
  • 2 tablespoon Flour
  • ½ teaspoon Baking powder
  • 1 Egg- lightly beaten

Kheer Rice

Slow-simmered rice pudding made with basmati rice, whole milk, and fragrant cardamom, studded with golden raisins and crunchy nuts. A warm, velvety dessert that tastes like a hug from grandma.

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup basmati rice, triple rinsed
  • Water, to cover the rice
  • 1 pinch saffron
  • 4 cups plus 1 Tbsp whole (3.25%) milk, divided
  • ½ Tbsp rose water

Fruit custard

Silky vanilla custard layered with juicy seasonal fruits—mangoes, strawberries, bananas, and pomegranate—topped with crunchy nuts and a drizzle of honey. Serve chilled for the ultimate no-bake dessert that’s both light and luxurious!

Ingredients

  • 2½ cups milk ((can use almond milk or soya milk))
  • 3 tablespoons custard powder ((or cornstarch or arrowroot powder))
  • ¼ cup warm milk ((to make dissolve custard powder))
  • 4 to 5 tablespoons sugar ((adjust to taste))
  • 1 medium apple

Shahi Tukda

A vibrant, one-pot medley of seasonal vegetables simmered in aromatic spices and tomatoes—healthy, hearty, and packed with flavor! Perfect with roti or rice.

Ingredients

  • 5 tablespoon ghee (clarified butter)
  • 7 slices bread
  • 5 cups milk
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup thick cream (fresh cream or table cream)

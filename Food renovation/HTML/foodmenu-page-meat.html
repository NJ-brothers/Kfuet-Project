Meat Food

Beef Nihari

Fall-apart tender beef shank simmered overnight in a fragrant masala gravy of fennel, mace, and slow-cooked marrow. Served with ginger slivers, fresh coriander, and warm tandoori naan for the ultimate Mughlai feast.

Ingredients

  • 6 Tail Pepper (Long pepepr, pipli)
  • 1 tbsp Black Pepper corns
  • 3 Black Cardamom
  • 8 Green Cardamom
  • 2 Cinnamon Sticks

Mutton Karahi

Succulent lamb pieces seared in a blistering iron karahi with ginger, garlic, and ember-red chilies—finished with crushed coriander and a squeeze of lemon. No cream, no frills, just pure meat magic scooped with steaming naan.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup oil or ghee
  • 1.1 lb lamb or mutton (bone in preferably)
  • 5 tomatoes, diced very finely
  • 1 bulb garlic (minced)
  • 1 thumb sized chunk of ginger (minced)

Seekh Kebab

Juicy ground beef (or lamb) blended with ginger, garlic, green chilies, and aromatic spices, molded onto skewers, and grilled to charred, smoky perfection. Serve with mint chutney, onions, and warm naan for the ultimate street food experience at home.

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp coriander seeds
  • 2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 small to medium yellow onion (roughly chopped)
  • 4 small green chili peppers such as Thai Chili (sliced)
  • 1/4 cup cilantro leaves

Karahi Chicken

Tender chicken pieces sizzled in a scorching iron karahi with tomatoes, ginger, garlic, and a double dose of green chilies—finished with crushed coriander and a swirl of butter. No cream, no shortcuts, just pure Pakistani street food energy meant to be devoured with garlic naan.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams Chicken
  • ¼ cup Curd
  • 1 teaspoon Ginger Garlic Paste
  • ¼ teaspoon Turmeric
  • ½ teaspoon Salt

Hyderabadi kadai

Succulent chicken simmered in a fragrant tomato-onion gravy infused with black cardamom, star anise, and freshly ground kadai masala—finished with crispy fried onions and a splash of lemon. A Nizami classic best paired with fluffy rumali roti or steamed rice.

Ingredients

  • 500 gm mixed chicken
  • 1 cup mint leaves
  • 1 cup fresh yogurt (curd / dahi)
  • 1 cup grated coconut (nariyal)
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste

Chicken Biryani

Layers of fragrant basmati rice and tender chicken marinated in yogurt and spices, slow-cooked to perfection with caramelized onions, fresh herbs, and a touch of saffron. Every bite is a harmony of flavors and textures—savory, aromatic, and utterly irresistible.

Veg Food

Mix Vegetable Curry

A vibrant, one-pot medley of seasonal vegetables simmered in aromatic spices and tomatoes—healthy, hearty, and packed with flavor! Perfect with roti or rice.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup oil or ghee
  • 2 medium onions (chopped)
  • 5 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tbsp minced ginger
  • 2 green chillies (whole)

Bhindi ki sabzi

Golden-fried okra tossed with cumin, turmeric, and a kick of red chili—no slime, just crunch! A 20-minute vegan delight that pairs perfectly with dal-rice or roti.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg okra
  • 1 large red onion
  • 2-3 green chilies (or as per taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2 tbsp coriander powder

Aloo Palak-Spinach

A vibrant, one-pot medley of seasonal vegetables simmered in aromatic spices and tomatoes—healthy, hearty, and packed with flavor! Perfect with roti or rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium aloo (potato) ((200-220 grams))
  • ½ lb. palak (spinach) ((~250 grams))
  • 2 big cloves garlic
  • 1 medium onion ((~80 grams))
  • 1 fresh chili (red or green)

Aloo Gobi Masala

Golden potatoes and cauliflower florets roasted with turmeric, cumin, and ginger—simple, satisfying, and packed with home-style flavor. Perfect with buttered roti or steaming basmati rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 head cauliflower, cut into 1-inch florets
  • 3 potatoes, peeled and cut into 1-inch chunks
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2 tomatoes, diced

Turnip Curry

Tender turnip cubes simmered in a golden onion-tomato gravy with cumin, coriander, and a hint of jaggery—a humble veggie turned soul-warming delight. Best scooped up with flaky parathas!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kg turnips
  • 1 tbsp Desi ghee (vegans can substitute with coconut oil)
  • 3 tbsp canola oil
  • 1/2 tsp cumin (zeera)
  • 180 g (~2 medium) onions (finely chopped)

Kadhi Pakora

A vibrant, one-pot medley of seasonal vegetables simmered in aromatic spices and tomatoes—healthy, hearty, and packed with flavor! Perfect with roti or rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups yogurt (full fat)
  • ½ cup garam flour ((besan))
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 2 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 2 green peppers (chopped)
